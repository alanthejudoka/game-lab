The game is played between 2 players (Red & yellow) and the objective of this game to have four squares in a row in your color.

The game starts with Red player making the first move by clicking on any square on the bottom row. And then the yellow player makes his move.

Pieces can only be placed on top of other pieces or on the bottom row.
